Drug use was falling, but that seems to be changing

Matt Ashby · 30 April 2019

Self-reported drug use has fallen for most of the past 20 years, but in the past six years the use of cannabis, cocaine and ecstasy has increased, particularly among people aged 16–24. However, drug use is still lower now than it was at the turn of the millenium.

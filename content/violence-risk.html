--- title: For women, attackers are more likely to have a familiar face author: Matt Ashby date: '2019-04-30' draft: true slug: violence-risk tags: - CSEW - crime - violence - sex ---

Men of all ages are more likely to be attacked by strangers than by people they know. Women have a lower risk of being victims of violence than men do, but (except before age 25) are as likely to be attacked by an acquaintance as by a stranger.
